Policy Statement

The commission will accomplish its mission in the following ways:

  • Research: The Commission will analyze and study the factors that prevent residents of Milwaukee County, Wisconsin (the "County") from participating fully in our social and economic institutions and from promoting healthy and safe communities.
  • Fostering Collaboration:The Commission will work in partnership with public and private institutions, community groups and low-income people to effectively fulfill community needs and minimize duplication of services.
  • Planning:The Commission will recommend solutions for the County‚Äôs social problems, plan for their implementation, coordinate efforts and provide technical assistance to community groups to solve these problems.
  • Advocacy:The Commission will advocate for greater participation in community affairs and create ways to help the economically or otherwise disadvantaged achieve self-sufficiency and independence.
  • Program Incubation and Operation:In cooperation with other governmental and non-governmental organizations, the Commission will pilot, launch, and operate services intended to achieve its mission.
  • Program Evaluation:The Commission will analyze, evaluate, and monitor the effectiveness of programs designed to accomplish its mission.
  • Human Relations:The Commission will actively work to fight racism and build an inclusive society.
